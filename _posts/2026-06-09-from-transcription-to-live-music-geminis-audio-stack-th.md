## Overview
This video features Thor Schaeff from Google DeepMind, showcasing the capabilities of Gemini's audio stack, which enables advanced audio understanding and generation. The demo highlights the power of Gemini's technology in transcription, speech generation, and music creation. This matters because it demonstrates the potential of AI in revolutionizing the way we interact with audio content.

## Key Insights
- Gemini's audio understanding layer can provide speaker labels, timestamps, emotion tags, language detection, and full summaries with a single API call.
- The Gemini 3.1 Flash Live model can perform sound-to-sound real-time multimodal processing with thinking baked in.
- The model can generate speech directed by a "director's note" rather than picking from a catalogue.
- Lyria 3, Google DeepMind's music generation model, can produce full songs with lyrics.
- Gemini Live can call Lyria via tool use to generate music on request.
- The demo showcases the generation of a German techno schlager about the UK startup scene, live on stage.

## Description

One API call to Gemini 3 Flash Preview: speaker labels by name, timestamps, emotion tags, language detection with English translation, and a full summary. That is the audio understanding layer that underlies everything else Thor Schaeff demos here, including speech generation directed by a "director's note" rather than picked from a catalogue, and Gemini 3.1 Flash Live, a sound to sound real time multimodal model with thinking baked in rather than cascaded through a separate LLM.

The talk ends with Lyria 3, Google DeepMind's music generation model that can now produce full songs with lyrics. The live demo has the Gemini Live model calling Lyria via tool use on request to generate a German techno schlager about the UK startup scene, live on stage.
